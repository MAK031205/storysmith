# Brag Plan: Storysmith

## What is this app?
Storysmith is a multi-agent narrative ideation system that turns simple behavioral observations into deep, contradictory NPCs for game writers.

## The angle
We highlight the transition from raw, simple observation to a complex, game-ready character. We lean into a clean, dramatic, and intellectual tone (polished/cinematic). The video opens in a monospace coder-style terminal to anchor the "how it works" before transitioning into a polished character output preview.

## Hook (first 2-3 seconds)
A sharp, raw input prompt being typed out letter by letter on a dark, terminal screen:
`> A teenager who always pays with exact change sorted by year, oldest first...`

## Key moments (the middle)
- **Scene 2 (3-7s):** The execution. Text lines flash quickly representing the multi-agent pipeline steps: `[Extractor] Raw input...`, `[Researcher] Topics...`, `[Theme Analyzer] Themes...`
- **Scene 3 (7-15s):** The reveal. A clean, beautiful UI card showcasing the generated character data: "Name: Kabir, 17", "Signature: Lines coins chronologically...", "Contradiction: Terrified of attention, yet counting out loud draws eyes."

## Outro / punchline
- **Scene 4 (15-20s):** Outro card with high-impact centered typography:
  "Storysmith. Better NPCs through structured reasoning."

## User flow worth showing
`Input observation prompt` -> `Pipeline analysis (Extractor, Researcher, Theme Analyzer)` -> `Character JSON profile output`.

## Tone
- Preset: cinematic
- Creative direction: polished narrative engine trailer
- Interpretation: Restrained motion, large elegant typography, dark background, sharp timing synchronization.

## Format: landscape — 1920x1080
## Duration: 20s

## Visual identity (from the project)
- Background: `#0B0C10` (Dark, near-black slate)
- Accent: `#66FCF1` (Neon teal/cyan)
- Text: `#C5C6C7` (Soft light gray)
- Display font: Outfit
- Body font: Fira Code (monospaced)
- Strongest visual element: Monospace terminal output transitioning into card-based JSON fields.

## Share copy (draft)
Storysmith: A multi-agent AI pipeline that helps writers discover richer, more contradictory NPCs through structured reasoning. Turn observations into deep characters.

## Audio direction
- Role: Cinematic support
- Music: `happy-beats-business-moves-vol-12-by-ende-dot-app.mp3` (dramatic/pulsing beat)
- Music treatment: Starts immediately, volume at 0.7, fades down to 0.2 during the outro logo.
- Music cue guidance: Detect cues at composition time using `beats` detection.
- Audio-reactive treatment: Subtle neon glow on the terminal typing and character card borders pulsing with the music RMS.
- SFX posture: Sparse. Keyboard typing ticks during Scene 1, a low hum/cyber sound for the pipeline flashing, and a clean card-flip sweep when the JSON profile pops in.
- Restraint rule: No generic ding/success sounds.

## Storyboard

### Scene 1 — The Prompt — 4.0s
A dark slate window representing a console. The prompt types out letter-by-letter:
`> A teenager who always pays with exact change sorted by year...`
- Sequential/interaction: yes (simulated keyboard typing)
- Audio intent: Mechanical, focused.
- Audio-coupled idea: Key click tick sounds matching the typing.
- Transition mood: Hard cut → Scene 2

### Scene 2 — The Pipeline — 4.0s
The terminal starts scrolling rapidly. Fast, flashing lines of logs:
`[Extractor] observational facts`
`[Researcher] human experience track`
`[Theme Analyzer] themes synthesized`
`[HITL] Creative Direction`
- Sequential/interaction: yes (rapid log updates)
- Audio intent: Computational, building tension.
- Audio-coupled idea: Low server hum/data-stream SFX.
- Transition mood: Dramatic wipe → Scene 3

### Scene 3 — The NPC Profile — 7.0s
A beautiful character card transitions in. Clean, structured text cards slide in sequentially:
- **Title:** `Kabir, 17`
- **Signature:** `Lines coins chronologically, oldest year first...`
- **Contradiction:** `Terrified of attention, yet his coin-counting ritual draws eyes.`
- Sequential/interaction: yes (cards slide in one-by-one on the beat grid)
- Audio intent: Satisfying reveal.
- Audio-coupled idea: Sleek card-slide/swoosh sounds.
- Transition mood: Soft crossfade → Scene 4

### Scene 4 — The Outro — 5.0s
The card fades out. Centered text fades in:
`Storysmith`
`Better NPCs through structured reasoning.`
- Sequential/interaction: none
- Audio intent: Decisive, lingering.
- Audio-coupled idea: Deep bass drop/reverb tail.
- Music mood for this video: cinematic
- Audio summary: Pulsing data-synth beat with typing clicks, transitioning into a swell and clean swooshes, ending on a low bass resonance.
